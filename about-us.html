Our Protocol Of Working

Project Dealing & Operating Processes

Our agency have a special protocol in dealing with projects with an organized way

Step One :

Collecting Data

Collecting data about the project is critical in order to ensure that the project meets your goals. By gathering information early on, you can avoid potential problems down the line. It's also important to be aware of your audience, and what they are likely to want and need from the project. By collecting data in an organized way, you can ensure that your project meets everyone's needs.

Step Two :

Making a plan & timetable

To be a great project manager, you have to have a plan and stick to it. Making a plan for the project is crucial because it helps you to measure everything. The plan also prevents you from being overwhelmed with the project process. The plan acts as a guideline on what needs to be done and when it should be done by. If your not prepared for the task at hand, you can find yourself in danger of losing important deadlines.

Step Three :

Start testing the project quality and result

Here at our company we have a different approach to the quality and the result of our products. We want to make sure that our project is handed perfectly rated so we always test it before we hand it to the client. Our tests include the checking of different criteria and things like the right color, right fit and right pattern, the right size, etc. Then we make sure that it has all the materials needed for a perfect result.

Step four :

Last step is handling the project to the client with national results

The last step is handing the final project to the client. When we finish the project, we test it and make sure it works correctly. Next, we send a report to our client telling them that their project is done and that there are no errors. Also, we tell them how much they will pay at the end of the predefined deadline. We also send a contract with all the details about the project to our clients. If everything goes well, we get paid for our work, and our client is happy with our work.
