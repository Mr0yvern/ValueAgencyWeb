Step Number 01.

Knowing Your Order

Knowing your client's order details and category is important. It allows you to create a successful project. If you are designing a website, be sure to know if it is a e-commerce website or not. If you are marketing a product, then know what that product is and where it will be going. These details can help you determine what services you will need in order to make everything happen. For example, designing a website without knowing that the site is an e-commerce site will make it difficult for you to determine what page layout is best for the client. Also, knowing the category of the project will help you choose services such as SEO and social media platforms that can help your client succeed with their business. Knowing these details beforehand will help you take on the best job possible and make a name for yourself as a professional designer or marketer.
